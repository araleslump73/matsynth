# Improvement: DAW Professional UI Parity (Multi-Device)

**ID:** IMP-001
**Type:** UX Improvement
**Priority:** High
**Status:** Delivered (PR open — manual review pending)

---

## Opportunity

**What are we improving?**

The current DAW interface should visually and functionally resemble a professional DAW layout, with stronger hierarchy, denser but readable track controls, and a modern timeline/piano-roll feel.

It must be fully usable on:
- Smartphone
- Tablet
- Desktop

Reference direction provided by stakeholder: professional DAW-style screenshot (arrangement + mixer paradigm).

**Why does this matter?**

- Product perception and trust strongly depend on visual/professional quality in music tools.
- A DAW-like interaction model reduces learning friction for musicians.
- Consistent multi-device behavior increases usability and session continuity.

Business impact declared by stakeholder at this stage: **no direct/critical immediate impact** (quality and positioning improvement cycle).
